CHICKENBBQ RECIPE

Filipino grilled chicken dish, particularly known in the Visayas region. It's a grilled chicken, often chicken thighs or legs, marinated in a flavorful blend of vinegar, calamansi juice, garlic, and lemongrass, and then grilled over hot coals.

How to Cook It?

Ingredients

Meat and Spices

  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp calamansi juice (or lemon/lime juice)

Procedure

  1. Prepare the Marinade
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ingredients Mix well until the salt is dissolved.
  3. Add 1 lb chicken thighs or legs (cut into bite-sized pieces) to the marinade.
  4. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or overnight for better flavor.
  7. Prepare for Grilling
  8. - Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  9. If using skewers, thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the skewers.
  10. If using banana leaves, wrap the chicken pieces in the leaves.
  11. Grill the Chicken
  12. Grill the chicken for 10-15 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  13. Baste the chicken with the marinade while grilling for extra flavor.
  14. Adjust the grilling time depending on the size of your chicken pieces.
  15. Serve
